WELCOME TO OUR SUPPORT CENTER

HOW CAN WE HELP YOU?

You can create a new bank account in our online web portal by providing your details. Once you register you will be provided with registration Id post which admin will verify your details and give you the access.

You will have to submit your photo, Aadhar card and pan card if available.

Once your account is created, account number and password will be generated. You can login through your email and password. Alternatively, you may call our Toll-Free customer care on 1800 1080 for assistance.

To change your password please log in to www.happiestmoney.com and navigate to Home > My profile > Profile > Click on Change Password.

You can change your Phone number, Email Id and any other personal details in the web portal, Please follow the following steps. Login > My profile > Profile > Edit > Save.
